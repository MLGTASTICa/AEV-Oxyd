bullet_data
/datum/bullet_data

You might ask why use a bullet data datum, and not store all the vars on the bullet itself, honestly its to keep track and initialize firing relevant vars only when needed This data is guaranteed to be of temporary use spanning 15-30 seconds or how long the bullet moves for. Putting them on the bullet makes each one take up more ram And ram is not a worry , but its better to initialize less and do the lifting on fire.
